About Me


I am Alexa, a professional in the field of HCI and UX.

Hi. I am a UX designer/UX researcher seeking opportunities to work in diverse environments. My interest in UX design stems from my passion for understanding the human mind and behaviors through the basis of my background in a beral arts education.
